Aural Training Game - Music Intervals!

Score

Created by Sinead Murphy 2021

Gameplay
Start game and listen to the two notes. Guess what the interval between them is, from the 12 options below. There are a possible 5 points for each interval. If you're not sure, you can skip and get no points, or you can guess and lose one point for each wrong guess.
Good Luck!!
